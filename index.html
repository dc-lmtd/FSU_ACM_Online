About

The Online ACM Club at FSU was founded in Spring 2020 with the purpose to provide a welcoming atmosphere, support students of all levels in their degree goals, and establish a hub for the online computer science community which builds community and connections with other students and professional associations.

Likewise, we want to provide members as much preparation and real-world experience toward their career goals by inviting a diverse guest speaker list, offering engaging coding challenges, and building team projects for various organizations, other school clubs, teachers, or for fun.

We promote teamwork, leadership, and integrity and aim to build a network of computer science professionals all over the United States for which to build lasting professional relationships.
